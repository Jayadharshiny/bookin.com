Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Are the books original and complete?

Yes, all books listed are original copies with all pages intact. No photocopies or pirated versions are sold here.

Q2: Are the books in good condition?

Yes, we only list books that are in readable and usable condition. Some may have names written or important points highlighted, which will be mentioned in the description.

Q3: How much discount do I get?

Most books are priced at 50% or less compared to the original price. You’ll find the exact price on each listing.

Q4: How can I place an order?

Just click the “Order Now” button under the book you’re interested in. You can also fill the form or contact us on WhatsApp.

Q5: Do you deliver books?

Yes, we deliver books via courier service across India. Currently, we operate from Tamil Nadu, and once you place an order, we'll contact you for shipping details.

Q6: What are the payment options?

At the moment, we accept Google Pay (GPay) only. Payment details will be shared with you after order confirmation.

Q7: Can I return or exchange a book?

Since these are second-hand books, returns are not accepted unless there's a major issue we missed. Please check book details carefully before ordering.

Q8: Can I sell my books on this website?

Yes! We’re planning to open seller listings soon. Stay tuned or contact us to know more.

Q9: Is it safe to order through this site?

Absolutely. We value your privacy and only use your contact info to process your order. No third-party sharing, ever.
